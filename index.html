Islam is a complete way of life that teaches peace, justice, and compassion.
It guides humans to worship one God, Allah, and follow His commandments.
The Holy Quran is the final book of guidance for all mankind.
Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) is the last messenger of Allah.
Islam emphasizes honesty, kindness, and respect for others.
It teaches us to help the poor and care for the needy.
Prayer (Salah) is an important pillar that strengthens faith.
Fasting in Ramadan builds patience and self-control.
